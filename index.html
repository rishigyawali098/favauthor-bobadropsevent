About Franz Kafka

Franz Kafka was a German-speaking writer born in 1883 in Prague. He is known for writing stories that show confusion, fear, and the struggles of individuals in a strange and unfair world.

Famous Books

The Metamorphosis

This story is about a man who wakes up one day transformed into an insect. It explores isolation, family pressure, and identity.

The Trial

A man is arrested without knowing his crime. The novel shows the confusion and fear caused by powerful systems.

The Castle

This book follows a man trying to gain access to a mysterious castle. It represents bureaucracy and frustration.
